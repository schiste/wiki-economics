# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should stay respectful, direct, and constructive. Contributors are expected to discuss technical disagreements in good faith and focus on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful in reviews, issues, and discussions
- criticize ideas, not people
- provide evidence for technical claims
- accept correction when something is wrong
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- insulting or demeaning comments
- deliberate disruption of discussions or review
- publication of private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers using the contact information available on the repository profile.
